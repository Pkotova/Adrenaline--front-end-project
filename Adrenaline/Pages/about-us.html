About us: Missions and Visions

The Adrenaline Center enhances the quality of life of people seeking adrenaline through exceptional outdoor adventure activities. The successful programs the ASC provides are inclusive to families and friends, empower our participants in their daily lives. Our staff is of the highest quality and is viewed as our most important asset. We believes that a professional, skilled staff is the ultimate key in providing an experience of premier quality for our participants.

On the Edge: First Rock Climbing

I went climbing with the the help of Adrenaline and had an amazing experience. I had never climbed before and I was quite nervous. From the moment I met my guide I felt secure. There were climbs of all difficulties and my guide did a great job at finding the appropriate climb for me. I took a half day experience course and I was able to climb multiple times during my four hour course. A great cliff, a great company. I highly recommend it.
